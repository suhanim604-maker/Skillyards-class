Classic Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch. Simple ingredients, quick instructions, and guaranteed comfort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
